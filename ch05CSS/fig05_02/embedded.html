Deitel & Associates, Inc.

Deitel & Associates, Inc. is an internationally recognized corporate training and publishing organization specializing in programming languages, Internet/World Wide Web technology and object technology education. The company provides courses on Java, C++, Visual Basic, C#, C, Internet and World Wide Web programming, Object Technology, and more.

Clients

The company's clients include many Fortune 1000 companies, government agencies, branches of the military and business organizations. Through its publishing partnership with Prentice Hall, Deitel & Associates, Inc. publishes leading-edge programming textbooks, professional books, interactive web-based multimedia Cyber Classrooms, satellite courses and DVD and web-based video courses.
